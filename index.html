Tournament Rules

General Rules

Minimum Age & Level

Players must be at least 13 years old. For solo matches, players must have a minimum game level of 30 to participate.

Fair Play

Any form of cheating, hacking, modding, or exploiting bugs will result in an immediate permanent ban and forfeiture of all winnings.

Room Joining

Players must join the game room within 5 minutes of the scheduled start time. Late entries will be disqualified without refund.

Screenshot Proof

All match results must be submitted with a clear screenshot showing the final score or winner within 30 minutes of match completion. Blurry or cropped screenshots may be rejected.

Host Responsibility

The host must provide the Room ID and password within 5 minutes after the match becomes full. Failure to do so will result in match cancellation and refunds to all players.

Disputes

Any disputes must be reported via the "Report a Problem" section in your profile within 24 hours. Include screenshots as evidence.

Entry Fee & Prizes

Entry fees must be paid before joining. Winners must submit screenshot proof to claim prizes. Prize distribution is handled only by the admin.

Account Rules

Only one account per player is allowed. Multi-accounting or account sharing will result in disqualification.

Behavior & Conduct

Respect all participants, hosts, and admins. Abusive language or inappropriate behavior may result in removal from the tournament.

Admin Decision

All decisions made by admins regarding matches, disputes, or cheating are final. Participants must comply.

Free Fire Rules

Match Type & Map

Matches will be Battle Royale (Solo/Duo/Squad) with maps selected by the host.

Room Access

Players must join the correct Room ID and Password shared by the host after match is full.

No Cheats

Use of any cheats, scripts, or hacks is strictly prohibited and results in immediate ban.

PUBG Mobile Rules

Match Type & Map

Matches follow Classic Mode. Maps and team size will be decided by the host.

Fair Play

Players must not use hacks, mods, or exploit bugs. Violators will be banned.

Room Joining

Players must join the correct Room ID on time to avoid disqualification.

Mobile Legends Rules

Match Mode

Matches will be Standard Ranked/Unranked, as per tournament announcement.

Hero Selection

Matches will be Standard Ranked/Unranked, as per tournament announcement.

Hero Selection

Players must follow hero selection rules provided by the host. No account sharing allowed.

COD Mobile Rules

Match Mode

Matches may include Multiplayer or Battle Royale as per host. Only original accounts allowed.

No Third-Party Software

Using hacks, mods, or scripts is strictly prohibited. Screenshots may be required for verification.

Terms & Conditions

Eligibility

You must be at least 18 years old to participate. Only one account per user is allowed. Duplicate accounts will be banned and all winnings forfeited.

Payments & Withdrawals

All payments are final. Withdrawals are processed daily at 10:00 PM. Minimum withdrawal amount is NPR 40. Withdrawals may take 24-48 hours to reflect in your account.

Platform Fee

A 5% platform fee is deducted from the winner's prize. The remaining amount is credited to the winner's winning balance and can be withdrawn.

Account Security

You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account. We are not liable for any loss due to unauthorized access.

Modification

We reserve the right to modify these terms at any time. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the new terms.

Contact

For any queries, contact support at support@adprotournament.com or via the complaint form in your profile.
